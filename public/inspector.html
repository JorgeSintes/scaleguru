Inspector

Inspector mode

The inspector mode is a straightforward tool designed to help musicians explore and understand musical scales and keys.

It's ideal for beginners seeking to familiarize themselves with scales or for more experienced players looking to review the more complicated scales. Simply select a key and scale to visually examine its structure and practice at your own pace.

Ready for more? Switch to Practice Mode to test your skills!
